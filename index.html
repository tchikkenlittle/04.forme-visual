About Jonas Keller

I'm Jonas Keller, founder of FORME Visual. Based in Berlin, I create clean, elevated product photography for DTC fashion and apparel brands. My approach combines architectural composition with an obsessive attention to texture and form — helping brands present their collections with clarity and confidence.
